    • CapCook Privacy And Policy

    Effective Date: December 25, 2024



    At CapCook, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit or make a purchase from our online store. By accessing and using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.


    1. Information We Collect
    To provide a seamless shopping experience, we collect the following types of information:


    a. Personal Information
    When you create an account, make a purchase, or interact with us in any way, we may collect:


    • Name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Shipping and billing address
    • Payment information (credit/debit card details, etc.)
    • Order history


    b. Usage Data
    We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including:


    • IP Address
    • Browser type
    • Device type
    • Pages visited on our site
    • Time spent on the site
    • Referral source (how you arrived at our site)


    This information is collected using cookies and similar tracking technologies.


    c. Payment Information
    We use a secure third-party payment processor for transactions. We do not store your full payment information (credit card numbers, etc.) but may retain transaction details for order processing and customer service purposes.



    2. How We Use Your Information
    We use the information we collect for the following purposes:


    To Process Transactions: We use your personal information to process and fulfill orders, including sending confirmations and receipts.

    To Improve Customer Service: Your information helps us respond more effectively to your requests and provide better customer service.

    To Personalize Your Experience: We may use the data to customize your shopping experience, offering product recommendations based on your preference.

    To Send Marketing Communications: With your consent, we may send promotional emails about new products, offers, or news. You can opt-out at any time.

    To Improve Our Website: We analyze how users interact with our site to improve user experience, content, and functionality.



    3. How We Protect Your Information
    We implement a variety of security measures to ensure the safety of your personal information when you make a purchase or enter personal data:


    Encryption: We use SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technology to protect sensitive information during transmission.

    Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to your data.

    Payment Security: All payments are processed securely by third-party payment processors. We do not store full payment details on our servers.While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security due to the inherent risks of online transactions.



    4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
    We use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to enhance your shopping experience, analyze site usage, and deliver targeted advertising. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you during subsequent visits. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though disabling cookies may impact your ability to use certain features of our website.



    5. Sharing Your Information
    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following situations:


    Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party companies that assist with website operations, payment processing, shipping, marketing, and customer service.

    Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to a subpoena or investigating fraudulent activities.

    Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of CapCook, your personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction.



    6. Your Rights and Choices
    You have several rights regarding your personal data:


    Access and Correction: You can request to view, update, or correct your personal information by logging into your account or contacting us directly.

    Data Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to any legal or contractual obligations.

    Opt-Out of Marketing: You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.

    Cookie Preferences: You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.



    7. Third-Party Links
    Our website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by CapCook. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.



    8. Children's Privacy
    CapCook is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.



    9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

      What is CapCook?

      CapCook is your one-stop online store for a wide variety of delicious desserts, foods, and refreshing drinks. We bring the taste of quality, freshly made products right to your doorstep.

      How can I place an order?

      Simply browse through our categories (Desserts, Foods, Drinks), select your items, and proceed to checkout. We offer multiple payment options for your convenience.

      What payment methods do you accept?

      We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods.

      Do you offer delivery?

      Yes, we deliver to various locations. Delivery fees may vary based on your location. You can check our delivery options during the checkout process.

      Can I cancel or modify my order?

      Orders can be canceled or modified within 30 minutes of placing them. After this period, we cannot guarantee any changes.
